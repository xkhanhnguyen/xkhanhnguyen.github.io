Health and Policy Analysis Team (HPAT)

Jul 2020 - Dec 2020

HPAT is co-founded by Havard and Columbia students. The research team conducts a data-driven study, under faculty advisors, on public and global health concerns.

Aim at:

  • Conducting and disseminating ethnographies of essential workers' experience of the COVID-19 pandemic, especially members of marginalized groups.
  • Focusing on evaluating the effects of policy decisions on essential workers to develop a critique of federal and state government responses to COVID-19 as well as contributing to labor advocacy efforts.
